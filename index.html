WHO WE ARE

NKL Restaurant established in 1950, stands tall in the bustling streets of Namakkal, with a legacy of over 70 years.

NKL Restaurant, established in 1950, stands tall in the bustling streets of Namakkal with a legacy over 60 years. Over the decades, each branch is celebrated for its impressive range of cuisines, including Chinese, Indian, Turkish, and Arabian, that add an exotic twist to the menu.
Behind the scenes, a team of highly skilled and experienced chefs, supported by a dedicated management team, work hard to keep up the top-notch standards that it is known for.
